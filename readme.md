## About

**Cybersecurity Architect** at a leading financial institution in Kuwait. I build open-source security tools, incident response playbooks, and compliance frameworks to strengthen cybersecurity across the Middle East.

**Education** — Carnegie Mellon University, Kuwait University, GUST University

**Certifications** — 8x GIAC | SANS LDR514 | SANS SEC530 | MCT | PCI DSS Professional

**Focus Areas:**
- Banking & Financial Security — PCI DSS, SWIFT CSP, CBK CORF
- ICS/IoT/OT Hardening — IEC 62443, NIST 800-82, NERC CIP
- Offensive Security — Penetration Testing, Red Teaming, CTF
- Zero Trust Architecture — Continuous Verification & Least Privilege
- Threat Hunting — KQL, SIEM, MITRE ATT&CK Mapping
- Community — Arabic cybersecurity content & mentorship
